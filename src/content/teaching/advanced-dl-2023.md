# CS 6787: Advanced Deep Learning

**Fall 2023** | University Name

## Course Description

Graduate-level course covering cutting-edge deep learning research, including transformers, generative models, self-supervised learning, and neural architecture search. Emphasizes paper reading, implementation, and research projects.

## Topics Covered

- Modern architectures (transformers, NAS, efficient models)
- Generative models (GANs, diffusion, LLMs)
- Self-supervised learning (contrastive, masked modeling)
- Robustness and efficiency (adversarial training, compression)
- Theoretical foundations (optimization, generalization)

## Assessment

- Assignments: 30%
- Research Project: 50%
- Paper Presentations: 15%
- Reading Responses: 5%
